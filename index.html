About Me

Makeup, character, transformation

Hi, I'm Dana — a makeup artist and prosthetics specialist based in Downtown Toronto. I'm currently studying at the Canadian Make-up Institute (CMU), deepening my craft across every discipline I love.

My work spans fashion, theatre, film and TV, prosthetics, and creature design. I'm drawn to the full range — from a clean editorial look to a fully sculpted character build — and I love the problem-solving that comes with each project.

I'm always looking to collaborate, learn, and grow. If you have a project in mind, I'd love to hear about it.
